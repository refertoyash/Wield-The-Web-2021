FAQs

  • Is the there any entry fee to be a part of this event?

    No, the event is free of cost.

  • How much time will I get to submit the website?

    You’ll get at least 4 – 5 days to submit the website.

  • Do I have to make a completely dynamic website?

    You need to make a template that is a single-page website.

  • How many rounds are there in the event?

    There will be two rounds in the contest. The first one will be a task round in which you have to just solve the task given in class. The second one will include an online quiz and template making.

  • What will be the overall marking scheme of this event?

    Your overall rank will be judged on three bases.

    ROUND-1

    1)You'll be given three tasks during your web development classes (30 marks)

    ROUND-2

    2)Online quiz consisting of 30 MCQ based ques (30 marks)
    3) You have to create and submit a template to prove your learning. (40 marks)

    TOTAL - 100 marks

  • Why should we participate in this event?

    Participating in such events improves your coding skills and helps you to explore new technologies.

  • How many questions will the quiz comprise? Which topic will they be?

    There will be a total of 30 questions based on HTML, CSS, Javascript, Git, Github and Bootstrap.

  • I do not know much about Git and Github. Can I still participate?

    Yes, of course. You don’t need to worry about it, our team will organize a dedicated session on Git and GitHub.
